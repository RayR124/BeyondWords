If you are just visiting, or still considering a premium membership, please consider the following:

    Our platform offers you the opportunity to join an affordable monthly subscription that enables you to compose and store heartfelt messages to your loved ones. Within our secure database, you can create a personalized list of individuals, complete with their mailing addresses, and assign a unique message to each person.
    When the time comes and your membership with our service ends, your letters to all designated recipients will be sent out in physical form. This means you can share your final words of wisdom with your children, express your deep love and farewells to your spouse, or even convey messages of resentment or reconciliation to your rivals.
    The service is incredibly cost-effective, with a minimal fee of approximately one dollar per person listed per month.
    Additionally, we offer a premium membership that grants you the ability to write longer letters, include more recipients, and even opt for additional services such as sending flowers alongside your letters.
    To make it even more convenient, we plan to partner with various insurance carriers to seamlessly integrate our fees into your existing bills. This way, you won't have to worry about separate payments and can focus on cherishing these meaningful connections.
